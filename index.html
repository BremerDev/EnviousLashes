Amber Bremer
Esthetician & Lash Tech

Welcome to Envious Lashes & Esthetics by Amber, where healthy, beautiful skin and stunning lashes are just a treatment away. As a licensed esthetician and certified eyelash technician, I am dedicated to providing top-quality services that help you look and feel your best. Whether you're looking to enhance your natural beauty with lash extensions or want a personalized skincare treatment that leaves your skin glowing, I have you covered. My mission is to help every client achieve their beauty goals by offering a range of personalized services that are tailored to meet their unique needs and preferences.

As an eyelash technician, I use only the highest quality products and techniques to create flawless, natural-looking lash extensions that make your eyes pop. And as a skincare specialist, I provide a range of treatments, including facials, body treatments, and waxing, that leave your skin refreshed, rejuvenated, and glowing.

But my services are about more than just treatments. I believe in building strong client relationships based on trust, communication, and compassion. I take the time to listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and provide education on proper skincare practices that you can incorporate into your daily routine.

So come on in and treat yourself to an experience that leaves you feeling relaxed, refreshed, and more beautiful than ever. Let's work together to help you achieve your beauty goals and feel confident in your own skin. Book your appointment today and experience the best in esthetics and lash services.
